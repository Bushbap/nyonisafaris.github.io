    Birding in Central and East Africa

    Central and Eastern Africa offer some of the most exciting birding experiences in the world, thanks to their vast and varied habitats and incredible birdlife. With over 1,500 bird species, including many endemic and near-endemic species, birding enthusiasts can look forward to seeing a wide range of birdlife in diverse landscapes, from lush rainforests to arid savannahs. Some of the top birding hotspots in the region include Uganda, which is home to the rare Shoebill and many other sought-after species, and Tanzania, which offers the chance to see the impressive East African Crowned Crane and other spectacular species. Other countries like Kenya, Rwanda, and the Democratic Republic of Congo also offer excellent birding opportunities, including the chance to see species like the African Grey Parrot, African Pitta, and Grauer's Broadbill. Come experience the thrill of birding in Central and Eastern Africa today!
