Three Night Kruger National Park Safari

$1000

Day 1: Kruger Mpumalanga International Airport to Kruger National Park

We leave Kruger Mpumalanga International Airport for you Kruger National Park Safari depending on your arrival time. The route to your Kruger safari will take about one hour until we reach Numbi Gate where we will sign into the park. After we will start our safari en route to our accommodation for the evening to check in.

Days 2 & 3: A Full Day in Kruger Park

Kruger Park safaris offer a thrilling opportunity to observe wildlife, with early morning departures that vary according to the seasons and camp gate opening times. Guests are provided with tea or coffee and snacks before embarking on a full-day drive. Expert guides with extensive knowledge of Kruger Park contribute to an exceptional safari experience with outstanding sightings. During the drive, we will stop at a Kruger picnic site for brunch. Depending on your booking, we may continue our game drive to our new camp, where we can relax and take a swim if the weather permits. We will take a short game drive until the gates shut, followed by dinner at 20h00.

Day 4: Kruger Park to Kruger Mpumalanga International Airport

We will start the day with a safari and have breakfast at one of the picnic sites. (Depending on your departure time at Kruger Mpumalanga International Airport) After breakfast we will depart for the airport exiting at Malelane Gate. *note : It is an hour drive back to Kruger Mpumalanga International Airport.
