about us

'Nyoni' means bird in the Shona language, representing freedom, adventure, and the natural beauty of Africa.

Hi! I'm Eugene your safari guide, and I want to help you create fun adventure. That journey of a lifetime, here in East and Southern Africa's vast wilderness. I want to help reconnect you with nature. Learn more about the unique birds, animals and plants that inhabit our stunning landscape and help actively play a part in its conservation.
