about us

'Nyoni' means bird in the Shona language, representing freedom, adventure, and the natural beauty of Africa.

Hi! I'm Eugene your safari guide, and I want to help you create fun adventure. That journey of a lifetime, here in East and Southern Africa's vast wilderness. I want to help reconnect you with nature. Learn more about the unique birds, animals and plants that inhabit our stunning landscape and help actively play a part in its conservation.

The number one rule on Safari is to have fun. Always full of surprises, Safaris are bursting to be unpacked. You just never know what amazing events are playing out around every corner. Majestic elephants wallowing in muddy, cooling pools, in the heat of the day. Impalas springing furtively across bush tracks in an adrenalin rush. Courageous 'David and Goliath' acts as little drongos dive-bomb giant eagles in mid-air, protecting their territory.
